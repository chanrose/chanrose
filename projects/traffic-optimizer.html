Traffic Optimizer

May 19, 2021 | Microcontroller | Watch Traffic Optimizer

Traffic Optimizer built upon Micropython and ESP32. Traffic Optimizer's inspiration is to create traffic lights that optimize performance whether there are vehicles passing or waiting for vehicles on other lanes, by minimizing the waiting time for vehicles in the red-light lanes.

What I used:

Micropython MQTT and Paho MQTT ESP32 RGB LED, HCSR04
