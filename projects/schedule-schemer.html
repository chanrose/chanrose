Schedule Schemer

May 19, 2021 | Web Application | Open Schedule Schemer

Schedule Schemer built upon Django + JQuery + Bootstrap. It is created to be a place where you get your auto-generated schedule and keeping track of your school curriculum. It is acheived by developing a module to generate class schedule for users in which also update when ever the administrator update the course detail.
